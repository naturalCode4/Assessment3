About

Adin is a 24 year old dude with over upper and lower extremities. These include hands and feet. Two of each of those to be extremely precise. Should we count the head as an extremity? Intersting thought; not sure... This year, Adin is focusing on deepening trust in himself and deepening his knowledge of the exact definition of 'extremity.' You know, come to think of it, its kind of a whack word for describing body limbs. They're not that extreme. I like to think of all parts of the body, no matter how distant from the center, as integral, unextreme, connected parts.
