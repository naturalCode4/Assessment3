The Neighborhood

Location

Adin lives in Frasier Meadows of South Boulder, CO.

The Vibes

Frasier Meadows is a quiet suburban area. The neighbors are mostly middle-aged folk, but Adin's main socialization is with his housemates and friends that come over.
